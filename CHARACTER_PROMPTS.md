# CHARACTER PROMPTS

## Ivan Lomov
- Describe a situation where Ivan struggles with making decisions. How does his indecisiveness affect those around him?
- Explore Ivan's relationship with his health. Is he genuinely worried about his ailments, or does he exaggerate them?  
- What are Ivan's values when it comes to love and marriage? Are they traditional or influenced by his social surroundings?

## Natalya Chubukov
- Detail Natalya's expectations of her suitors. What does she find appealing or unappealing in a partner?
- Analyze how Natalya's relationship with her father impacts her choices and behaviors in romantic relationships.
- How does Natalya react to conflict? Does she embrace or avoid confrontation?

## Stepan Chubukov
- Discuss Stepan's role as a father. What are his beliefs about traditional marriage?
- Explore how Stepan navigates social expectations in his dealings with opponents, like Ivan.
- What motivates Stepan to intervene in the relationship between Ivan and Natalya? Is it love for his daughter, social standing, or something else?
